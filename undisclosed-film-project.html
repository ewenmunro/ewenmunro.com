This film is currently in development

Genre:

Comedy, Drama

Logline:

A young actor who is searching for some greater meaning to her life takes on the role of a rude, obnoxious know-it-all in a film who over a series of vignettes devises ways to ‘teach the world a lesson.’

Short Synopsis:

A young actor who has recently returned home from vagabonding around the world in search for some greater meaning to her life plays the role of a rude, obnoxious know-it-all in a film. The film begins with her character having disdain for society, complaining and moaning over her particular circumstances, only for her animosity to inspire her to want to ‘teach the world a lesson.’ Her character teams up with her roommate as they both, over a series of vignettes, devise ways to ‘correct’ the world as they see it and, in turn, pave the way for a greater conflict.

Long Synopsis:

A young actor who has recently returned home from vagabonding around the world in search for some greater meaning to her life is cast in a film as the lead character, a rude, obnoxious know-it-all who cannot accept things not going her way. In the film, her character cannot stop complaining and moaning over her current occupation. Life to her is only misery, no matter what she does, and this inspires her to want to get back at the world, to ‘teach the world a lesson.’ She teams up with her only friend and roommate to wreak havoc on society, devising ways to ‘correct’ the world as they see it. But it all doesn’t go to plan, as they head towards an even greater conflict.
