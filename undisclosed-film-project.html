Status: In Development

This film is currently in development

Genre:

Comedy, Drama

Logline:

A young actor who is searching for some greater meaning to her life takes on the role of a rude, obnoxious know-it-all in a film who over one long night falls into a heated conflict with her roommate who has a vastly different perspective to hers.

Short Synopsis:

A young actor who has recently returned home from vagabonding around the world in search for some greater meaning to her life plays the role of a rude, obnoxious know-it-all in a film. The film begins with her character and her best friend and roommate arriving home heavily intoxicated from a night out on the town, only to make a large ruckus and wake up their down-to-earth, level-headed, reclusive roommate whom they look down on. This initial dispute paves the way for a much more heated conflict between the three characters as their different world views collide.

Long Synopsis:

A young actor who has recently returned home from vagabonding around the world in search for some greater meaning to her life is cast in a film as the lead character, a rude, obnoxious know-it-all who cannot accept things not going her way. In the film, her character arrives home with her best friend and roommate heavily intoxicated from a night out on the town and create a large ruckus that wakes up their other down-to-earth, level-headed, reclusive roommate whom they look down on and virtually ignore for being, in their eyes, different to them. Their reclusive roommate politely asks them to keep the noise down, only for them to ignore her request and continue to party on into the night. This stalemate forces them to have to confront one another, leading them to have to learn more about each other and this, in turn, paves the way for a much more heated conflict between the three characters as their different world views collide.
